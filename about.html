Hi. My name is Prateek Sahay.

I'm a robotics engineer working on autonomous aircraft at Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation, and I'm very passionate about robotics design engineering. I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute with two bachelor's degrees in Robotics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering.

In my free time, I practice web design, electronics, programming, and basic graphic design. Something that makes me unique is my strong belief in the marriage of the engineering sciences and vibrant arts. I'm attracted to environments where I learn new things often.

I also enjoy giving back to the community through volunteer work: I've performed magic shows, volunteered at the Museum of Science in Boston, and done graphic design work for Essence of India, an annual celebration of classical Indian culture and heritage in my hometown.

My interests are wide and ever-expanding — I enjoy reading, traveling, building things, photography, sports cars, sound systems, engineering, economics, philosophy, table tennis, hanging out with friends, and introspection. I'm also a big fan of usability and user experience design. For me, the purpose of life is to become the best possible version of myself and to make the people around me happy. I also believe robots can teach us a lot about what it means to be human. I dream of one day using my knowledge to create robots to aid humans.
