Hi. My name is Prateek Sahay.

I'm an aspiring undergraduate at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in Worcester, MA graduating this spring. I'm studying Robotics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering because I'm passionate about design engineering, and I dream of using my skills to create robots to aid humans.

I also practice web design, electronics, a few programming languages, and basic graphic design. Something that makes me unique is my strong belief in the marriage of the engineering sciences and vibrant arts. I also believe knowledge is power and try to make sure I'm learning something new often.

I also give back to the community through volunteer work: I've performed magic shows at events, worked at the Museum of Science in Boston, and done graphic design work for Essence of India, which is an annual celebration of classical Indian culture and heritage in my hometown.

My interests are wide and always expanding — I enjoy reading, traveling, building things, photography, sports cars, sound systems, engineering, economics, art, psychology, philosophy, hanging out with friends, and introspection. I'm also a big fan of usability and user experience design. For me, the purpose of my life is to become the best possible version of myself and to make the people around me happy. I also believe robots can teach us a lot about what it means to be human.
