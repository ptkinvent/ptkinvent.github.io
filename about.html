Hi. My name is Prateek Sahay.

I'm a robotics engineer working on autonomous aircraft at Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation, and I'm very passionate about robotics design engineering. I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute with two bachelor's degrees in Robotics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering.

In my free time, I practice web design, electronics, programming, and some graphic design. Something that makes me unique is my strong belief in the importance of mixing engineering sciences and vibrant arts. I'm attracted to environments where I learn new things often.

I also enjoy giving back to the community through volunteer work: I've performed magic shows, volunteered at the Museum of Science in Boston, and done graphic design work for Essence of India, an annual celebration of classical Indian culture and heritage in my hometown.

My interests are ever-expanding—I enjoy reading, traveling, building, photographing, driving, turning up sound systems, playing table tennis, and having chill sessions with friends. I'm also a fan of usability and user experience design.

About the Website

I designed this website on my own and coded it with the help of Twitter's Bootstrap and Jekyll. It was my first foray into HTML and CSS, and it's a continually enriching experience. Thanks to the powers of Bootstrap, this website is fully optimized for your mobile display as well. Enjoy!
