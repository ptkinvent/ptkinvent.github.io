Hi. My name is Prateek Sahay.

I'm an aspiring engineer at Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation. I graduated with my bachelor's degree at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in Worcester, MA in both Robotics Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. I'm also passionate about design engineering, and I dream of using my skills to create robots to aid humans.

In my free time, I practice web design, electronics, some hobby programming, and basic graphic design. Something that makes me unique is my strong belief in the marriage of the engineering sciences and vibrant arts. I also believe knowledge is power so I'm attracted to situations where I'm learning something new often.

I also enjoy giving back to the community through volunteer work: I've performed magic shows, volnteered at the Museum of Science in Boston, and done graphic design work for Essence of India, an annual celebration of classical Indian culture and heritage in my hometown.

My interests are wide and ever-expanding — I enjoy reading, traveling, building things, photography, sports cars, sound systems, engineering, economics, art, psychology, philosophy, hanging out with friends, and introspection. I'm also a big fan of usability and user experience design. For me, the purpose of my life is to become the best possible version of myself and to make the people around me happy. I also believe robots can teach us a lot about what it means to be human.
