With Microsoft's recent acquisition of GitHub, you may be feeling more motivated than ever to learn git. Finally, today's the day when you finally learn git! No more version control by keeping copies in Dropbox.

The first piece of good news is that you're in the right place. We're going to go through the most common commands one by one and, more importantly, when to use them. The second is that git is probably the right tool for your version control. It's incredibly powerful when used correctly, and will definitely help you when you have to collaborate on large software projects.
