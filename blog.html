Welcome to my Blog

They call it the curse of knowledge—when you've known something so long that you can't remember ever not knowing it in the first place. You become impatient, a bad teacher because you can't understand how anyone else could not understand something so basic.

I started writing software in college after growing up with several software developers. I felt pretty lost for a while (what's a Boolean?), and without a lot of guidance at home I didn't even know how to get started. I hope to use this blog as a way to document my journey since then, along with some of my biggest pitfalls in the hopes of inspiring someone else and providing the much-needed guidance a student needs.
