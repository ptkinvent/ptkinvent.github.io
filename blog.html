Welcome to my Blog

They call it the curse of knowledge: when you've known something for so long that you can't remember ever not knowing it in the first place. You become a bad teacher, impatient because you can't understand how someone else could have so much trouble with a basic concept.

I got started in software later than some of my friends, so I can appreciate how difficult the learning curve can be sometimes. I hope to use this blog as a way to document some of the hardest parts of my journey and in the hopes of helping someone else.
